Our mission

PawfectPuppick is dedicated to helping people find their ideal dog type through a fun and informative quiz. By matching individuals with the perfect breed, we aim to prevent dogs from ending up in shelters due to mismatched expectations. Our goal is to ensure every dog finds a loving, well-suited home where they can thrive and be cherished.

Our Achievements

We have helped many find their ideal dog breed. Our quiz ensures that your lifestyle can accomodate your dog. We also educate people about dog health and the various measures needed to take to ensure that you dog is growing in a healthy manner.We are commited to healthy pet ownership
